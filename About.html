Firm Overview

KISKAN LAW AFRICA LLP ADVOCATES is a young, dynamic, energetic and fast-paced law firm that was established following a merger between Kisabit & Associates (Est. 2012) and Mburugu & Kanyonge Associates, Advocates (Est.2014). It consists of brilliant and diverse legal practitioners, researchers, paralegals and interns.

The Firm specializes in commercial transactions, intellectual property, fintech technology, tax consultancy, Conveyancing, legislative drafting, legal audits and dispute resolution in public interest and constitutional litigation, labour relations, civil and commercial litigation.

The Firm provides professional legal services to individuals, financial institutions, fintechs, companies (private, public and foreign), state corporations, benefit schemes, NGO’s, Associations and Groups.

The Firm conducts business primarily in Kenya with partners within the region. Because of our diverse educational backgrounds, we have established and cultivated working relationships and networks with lawyers across the world.
